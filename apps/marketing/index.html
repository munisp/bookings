Platform

Everything a front desk needs — nothing you can’t audit

Services in Go, Rust, Python, and TypeScript. Every layer is open source and self-hostable.

Bidirectional Twenty CRM sync

Contacts, bookings, and conversations flow into a self-hosted Twenty CRM — forward event sync plus reverse webhooks with echo suppression.

30 industry vertical packs

From salon to law enforcement — each pack ships terminology, booking policy, agent persona, and workflow variants. Developing-market packs included.

Booking engine with payments

Live availability, deposits and payments at booking time, Temporal-saga durability with compensation, and idempotent commands end to end.

QR-code invoice payments

Customers scan and pay invoices by QR — Paystack payment links where available, with a static EMV merchant-payload fallback everywhere else.

Usage metering & billing

A Rust billing engine meters usage from Kafka events, rates it against plan quotas, issues invoices, and posts to a TigerBeetle-compatible double-entry ledger with Mojaloop rails.

Open-model AI, your hardware

Ollama Qwen3 by default with a MiniMax fallback, faster-whisper speech-to-text, Piper text-to-speech, and LiveKit for real-time voice.

GDPR & NDPA compliance

Compliance profiles for EU GDPR and Nigeria’s NDPA, row-level multi-tenant isolation in Postgres, and data-residency-friendly self-hosting.

Role-based KPI dashboards

Owner, admin, analyst, billing, staff, and viewer roles — backed by Keycloak and Permify — with white-label branding per tenant.

How it works

Live in three steps

  1. 1

    Pick your pack & brand it

    Choose from 30 vertical packs, apply your logo and colours with the white-label theme editor, and publish your booking page.

  2. 2

    Connect channels & CRM

    Switch on voice, WhatsApp, Telegram, web chat, and SMS — and sync everything bidirectionally with your self-hosted Twenty CRM.

  3. 3

    Let the AI front desk work

    The concierge answers questions, books with deposits, and takes QR payments — while role-based dashboards track bookings, revenue, and sentiment.
